GENERAL RULE

All these rules are applied to every categories on Sportherald which must be strictly adhere to in other to have predictions accepted. Failure to comply with all the rules will lead to immediate rejection of your predictions.

There will be stipulated leagues to be predicted and the numbers of games to predict will be listed in all categories,failure to strictly follow any of this will lead to immediate rejection of your predictions as well.

Predictions must only be on daily matches and any match that exceeds 10pm must not be included.predicting matches other than the stipulated time above will lead to immediate rejection of your predictions

Sportherald Referees/Gaffers have the full right to accept or reject predictions according to the stated rules in the categories.

Gaffers may revert an accepted / rejected predictions.

Guidelines to predictions
No writting of codes like 1x x x2 HWEH HSH and so on, you are advised to write as follows for example
manchester united vs chelsea

Manchester united win or chelsea win
manchester united lose or chelsea lose
manchester united draw or chelsea draw
manchester united 2goals or chelsea 2goals
manchester united more than 3goals or chelsea more than 3goals
manchester united win first half or chelsea win first half
and so
Receding votes and approval

  • Predictions found in violation of sportherald rules will be unvoted or rejected even after they have been accepted.

  • Erroneous (accidental) sportherald upvotes will be receded.

  • Bans and Downvotes

  • rules are made easy to allow predictors make easy predictions,so any form of ridiculous predictions made in other to abuse the system will be rejected immediately and such predictor might be ban from sportherald

  • Harassing any member of the sportherald team

  • Consistently predicting already played matches or matches in play even after being notified

  • Using multiple accounts to abuse the system.

  • Plagiarism.

  • Tag spamming.

Submission Originality

  • Duplicate predictions (by the same or different user) will be automatically rejected.
  • Content shared previously will be rejected if a referee discovers plagiarism, and the user predicting it will be banned from sportherald.

Submission Language
Only prediction posts written in plain and easily understood English will be accepted.

Defamation

  • comments must not include offensive speech directed at others.
  • comments may not contain false information about another user that may be perceived as defamatory.

Solicitation

  • predictors should not use prediction posts to solicit for any activity that is not strictly accepted by sportherald Rules.
  • predictors should not ask for Steem / Steemit engagement (upvotes, resteems and follows) in their prediction posts.
  • predictors should not ask for sportherald engagement (upvotes and follows) in their prediction posts.

Prediction Authorship

  • The prediction must provide the information necessary to verify the identity of the predictor as the post author.
  • If a predictor’s Steem / Utopian username does not match the username used in an external platform, they must either edit the username on the external platform, or provide alternative means of verification.
